Established in 1995, Impact Precision Sdn Bhd has been a market leader in Malaysia for hydraulic system and component supplies ever since.

With more than 20 years of experience, we are able to provide complete solution for hydraulic systems used in various industries such as mobile, construction, industrial machinery, marine, oil & gas, agriculture, refuse vehicles and more.

We represent some of the worldwide leading brands such as Parker, GPM, Brevini, Yuken, SAI and more, providing a complete range of hydraulic components.

Our Expertise

One Stop Solution

We are one of the largest hydraulic components stockist in Malaysia, stocking a huge range of hydraulic products

From pumps to motors, accumulators to coolers, valves to tank accessories, our complete range and strong stock position can definitely meet your needs and expectations.

Pump Assembly

Our cast iron pumps from GPM South Africa, and Aluminum pumps from Salami and Brevini (both from Italy), are suitable for huge range of applications.

With our rich experience and versatility, we are able to design custom made pumps with different physical specifications (standard or special) for any applications

Cylinder Fabrication

We are able to design and fabricate hydraulic cylinder (tie-rod, welded) based on your choice of mounting, materials, and seals. Our cylinders have been widely used in robust applications such as mobile & industrial.

Accumulator Service & Repairing

Having represented Parker Olaer for more than 20 years, we provide one-stop service for accumulator servicing. This includes Nitrogen gas precharing/refilling, and spart part replacements, such as bladder kit, gas valves, seal kit, anti-extrusion ring and fluid port assembly.

Power Unit Fabrication

Designing and fabricating power unit has been one of our strengths due to the rich experience of our technical staff, and our strong stock positions.

From electric motors to pumps, solenoid valves to pressure control valves, filters to coolers, a huge range of hydraulic components are always readily available
